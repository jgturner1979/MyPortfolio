About Me

I graduated from Southern Methodist University in December 2001 with a Bachelor of Arts in Advertising and a minor in Spanish. Over the last 17 years, I've spent the majority of my career working in marketing for companies in various industries; insurance, retail/retail grocery and real estate. In almost every position, I worked with web developers to maintain front-end and back-end web applications. In the last few years, with the emergence of WordPress, I've been able to use the web platforms CMS to make non-critical updates to a company's front-end website.

I am looking to switch careers from digital marketing to web development. My full stack web developer course will be completed in January 2019. The program has been great and I've expanded my programming language knowledge from HTML and CSS to include JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, React, MySQL, and MongoDB. I look forward to working with other developers to continue learning as I grow into this new career.
